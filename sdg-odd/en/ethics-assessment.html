Part 1 - Data Ethics Principles

BETA: This is a tool in development. We welcome your feedback.

Statistics Canada's core data ethics principles for data collection, editing and dissemination are evaluated through the following five elements:

  • 1. Benefit to Canadians
  • 2. Fairness and do no harm
  • 3. Transparency and accountability
  • 4. Privacy
  • 5. Confidentiality

Evaluating each of these elements helps us assess if the data are used appropriately throughout its life cycle and can therefore be considered suitable for the purpose of contributing to Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) reporting. Please consult Statistics Canada's Trust Center to learn more about data ethics.
