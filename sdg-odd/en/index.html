Data Suitability Assessment Tool

BETA: This is a tool in development. We welcome your feedback.

The Data Suitability Assessment Tool is mainly designed to help assess if non-official data are considered fit for purpose to contribute to Canada's reporting on the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), but it can also be used to evaluate data for any other purposes.

Through this tool, data suitability is assessed by evaluating both the quality and the ethics of the data.

  • Data ethics assess whether the data have been acquired and used appropriately throughout their life cycle, which spans from the acquisition to the dissemination.
  • Data quality is important as it helps evaluate the measures in place to safeguard the data against errors and inform users of their fitness for purpose.

Please note that this Data Suitability Assessment Tool is not meant to assess if the data meet official statistics standards.

If you wish to pursue the assessment of your data for potential SDG reporting once the data have been evaluated with this tool, or if you have any questions, please contact Statistics Canada's SDG team with your submission or inquiries.
